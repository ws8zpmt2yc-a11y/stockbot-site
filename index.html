HOW IT WORKS

Boring machinery, deliberately.

Five strategies, one ensemble

Blends five uncorrelated, long-only strategies — a volatility-targeted core, cross-sectional momentum, mean-reversion, and a volatility-spike buyer — across liquid ETFs (SPY, QQQ, TLT, GLD, XLU). Every trade is confidence-scored and the mix adapts to the market regime. Cash-only: no leverage, no shorting.

Stops that fire while you sleep

Every position carries a hard ATR-based stop from the moment it opens — losers get cut around −2% on average, automatically, at 3am or 3pm. Protection never needs you awake.

Cash only. Never leverage.

Position sizes are computed from cash on hand — never margin, never borrowed exposure. The one time a bug created accidental leverage, we unwound it, published the post-mortem, and hard-coded the guard.
