On your phone right now? StockBot is a Mac app — copy this page's link and open it on your Mac whenever you're ready. It verifies your purchase again and gives you a fresh download.

⚠️ When you first open StockBot, your Mac will say "Apple could not verify…" — that's normal for apps outside the App Store, and takes 20 seconds to fix (one time only):

2) Open System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down
3) Click Open Anyway next to StockBot
Older Macs: just right-click StockBot.app → Open → Open. Full steps are also inside your download ("OPEN ME FIRST").
